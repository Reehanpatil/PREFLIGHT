# PREFLIGHT

**Cleared for launch, or not yet.**

PREFLIGHT checks whether your project is actually ready for a [Product Hunt](https://www.producthunt.com) launch — before hunters and users see it first. Paste your GitHub repo, get a readiness score, a prioritized list of what to fix, and ready-to-paste launch copy — all in your browser.

## What it's for

Most launches don't fail because the product is bad — they fail because of avoidable, first-impression problems: no screenshot, no live demo link, a tagline that's too long, a README that says nothing. PREFLIGHT catches these before launch day, and hands you the fix instead of just pointing out the problem.

## How to use it

1. **Open the site** and paste your GitHub repository URL (e.g. `https://github.com/you/yourproject`).
2. *(Optional)* Add your landing page URL to also check your page title and meta description.
3. *(Optional)* Add your own Anthropic API key to get AI-written launch copy instead of the built-in template draft. Your key stays in your browser and is never stored or sent anywhere except Anthropic.
4. Click **Run pre-flight check**.
5. Review your results:
   - **Readiness score** and pass/warn/fail status
   - **Fix these first** — your highest-impact issues, each with a ready-to-copy fix (a starter README, a real LICENSE file, suggested tags, and more)
   - **Manifest checklist** — the full breakdown of every check
   - **Listing copy** — a tagline, description, and maker's first comment, ready to paste into your Product Hunt submission
6. Click **check gallery images** to drag in your planned Product Hunt gallery images and check them against the recommended 1270×760px size.
7. Copy your **README badge** to show your score on your project's README.
8. Scan again anytime — PREFLIGHT remembers your past scores locally and shows how much you've improved.

No sign-up, no account, no backend. Everything runs in your own browser.
